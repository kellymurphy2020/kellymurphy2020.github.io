Wind Speed


The wind is caused by differences in atmospheric pressure. When a difference in atmospheric pressure exists, air moves from the higher to the lower pressure area, resulting in winds of various speeds. On a rotating planet, air will also be deflected by the Coriolis effect, except exactly on the equator. There appears to be no significant trend or relation between latitude and wind speed, as we get closet to the equator.
